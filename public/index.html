About
BGSI market helper for bloxy.trade. Rate: $0.009–$0.010 per 1K value.

Pet Buying — WIN / FAIR / LOSE check on any price.
Pet Selling — suggested listing price.
Withdraw — fee calculator.
Deals — all pages aggregated. Filter by WIN, Near Win, etc.
Budget — knapsack optimizer. Auto mode tries every threshold.
Profile — click your avatar to view. Track pets, see P&L and value changes.
Settings — Discord webhook alerts, synced to your account.
